The region of perpetual snow in the north. It encompasses the Ilum Peninsula and all territories north of the Wall.

Neither the kingdoms nor the Empire have much influence here. There is no centralized authority; individual cities and settlements have their own governing bodies. 

It is sparsely populated, and settlements are mainly located along the coast. Traveling overland is quite difficult due to the harsh weather. Getting from one settlement to another requires a great deal of preparation and luck.

Much of the territory remains unexplored, or at least there are few records of it. More and more expeditions are being organized to explore the icy wilderness and tap into the potential of its undiscovered treasures.
