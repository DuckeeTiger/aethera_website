# The Worldveil


The **Worldveil** is the invisible force that holds the shattered world of Aethera together.

After the War of the Eidolons, the original world of **Racriaven** was destroyed. The continents, seas, and even reality itself began to break apart. The boundaries between planes collapsed, gravity became unstable, and parts of the world started drifting into the void.

In the final moments of the war, **Racelion** and **Viraen** sacrificed most of their remaining power to prevent total destruction. Instead of restoring the world, they created a metaphysical structure that could stabilize what remained.

This structure became known as the **Worldveil**.

The Worldveil surrounds and connects the celestial shards of Aethera, keeping them stable and habitable. It regulates:

- gravity,
- planar boundaries,
- magical flow,
- and the natural laws of reality itself.

Without the Worldveil, the shards of Aethera would slowly collapse into chaos or drift apart into the void.

---

### The Veilweave

Scholars and Aethermancers often refer to the inner structure of the Worldveil as the **Veilweave**.

The Veilweave is a network of invisible metaphysical energy flowing beneath reality. It connects the world together like threads in a massive cosmic web.

Many believe that **Aether** itself is created from stress or fractures within the Veilweave. This is why Aetherstone has unusual properties connected to gravity, space, and planar magic.

Because of this, powerful magic can damage the Veil:

- large portals,
- planar rituals,
- divine artifacts,
- and some forms of Aethermancy.

Most mortals do not fully understand the danger of this.

---

### Weak Points in the Veil

The Worldveil is not perfect. Some regions of Aethera are unstable where reality was heavily damaged during the ancient wars.

The largest known fracture is the **Aether Sea**, where the Material Plane overlaps with elemental and astral energies. Strange storms, magical anomalies, and temporal distortions are common there.

Other weak points can be found:

- near ancient dragon battlefields,
- around collapsed planar gates,
- deep beneath old ruins,
- and in places touched by Eidolon corruption.

In these regions, reality becomes thinner and less stable.
