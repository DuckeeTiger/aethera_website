## The Eternal Pair  
### _The First Gods of Creation_  
  
Before the birth of the pantheon, before dragons, mortals, or the shaping of the world itself, there were only two beings who stood against the endless cycle of destruction:  
  
**Racelion** and **Viraen**.  
  
Together they created the first world, **Racriaven**, and from their union came both divine order and primal creation. Though their visions often differed, their powers once existed in balance, shaping life, magic, and the foundations of reality itself.  
  
During the War of the Eidolons, their conflict nearly destroyed creation. In the final moments of the Shattering, they sacrificed their physical forms and merged their remaining essence into the **Worldveil**, the metaphysical structure that still binds the shattered world together.  
  
Most faiths no longer worship them as traditional gods. Instead, they are remembered as cosmic forces, the creators whose sacrifice allowed existence itself to survive.

## Outside the Balance
### _The Broken Powers_

These entities belong neither to Racelion nor Viraen.

Most scholars believe they emerged after the Shattering, born not from the original harmony of creation, but from mortal fear, suffering, and ambition.

Many see them as fractures within the divine order itself.
