> # Topple
> *Weapon Property*
>
> If you hit a creature with a weapon with the ***Topple*** property, you can force the creature to make a Constitution saving throw (DC 8 plus the ability modifier used to make the attack roll and your Proficiency Bonus). On a failed save, the creature has the Prone condition.
