> # Animal Friendship
>
> *Level 1 Enchantment ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Bard/Bard\|Bard]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]])*
>
>
> **Casting Time:** Action  
> **Range:** 30 feet  
> **Components:** V, S, M (a morsel of food)  
> **Duration:** 24 hours  
>
> Target a Beast that you can see within range. The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or have the Charmed condition for the duration. If you or one of your allies deals damage to the target, the spell ends.
>
> ***Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.*** You can target one additional Beast for each spell slot level above 1.
>
