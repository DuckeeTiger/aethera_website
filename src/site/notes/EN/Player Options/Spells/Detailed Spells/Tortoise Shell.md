> # Tortoise Shell
>
> *Level 2 Abjuration ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Artificer/Artificer\|Artificer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]])*
>
>
> **Casting Time:** Action  
> **Range:** Touch  
> **Components:** V, S
> **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute
>
> You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target’s skin hardens into a tortoise shell, and the target gains a +3 bonus to AC. If the target moves, the bonus becomes +1 instead until the start of its next turn.
>
