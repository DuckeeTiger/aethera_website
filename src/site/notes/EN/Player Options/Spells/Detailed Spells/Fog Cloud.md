> # Fog Cloud
>
> *Level 1 Conjuration ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Sorcerer/Sorcerer\|Sorcerer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])*
>
>
> **Casting Time:** Action  
> **Range:** 120 feet  
> **Components:** V, S  
> **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour  
>
> You create a 20-foot-radius Sphere of fog centered on a point within range. The Sphere is Heavily Obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a strong wind (such as one created by Gust of Wind) disperses it.
>
> ***Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.*** The fog’s radius increases by 20 feet for each spell slot level above 1.
>
