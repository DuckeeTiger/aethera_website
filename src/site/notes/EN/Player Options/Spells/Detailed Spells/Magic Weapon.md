> # Magic Weapon
>
> *Level 2 Transmutation ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Artificer/Artificer\|Artificer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Paladin/Paladin\|Paladin]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Ranger/Ranger\|Ranger]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Sorcerer/Sorcerer\|Sorcerer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])*
>
>
> **Casting Time:** Bonus Action  
> **Range:** Touch  
> **Components:** V, S  
> **Duration:** 1 hour  
>
> **You touch a nonmagical weapon.** Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls. The spell ends early if you cast it again.
>
> ***Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.*** The bonus increases to +2 with a level 3–5 spell slot. The bonus increases to +3 with a level 6+ spell slot.
