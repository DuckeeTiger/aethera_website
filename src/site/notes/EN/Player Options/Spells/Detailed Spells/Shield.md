> # Shield
>
> *Level 1 Abjuration ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Sorcerer/Sorcerer\|Sorcerer]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])*
>
>
> **Casting Time:** Reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack roll or targeted by the Magic Missile spell  
> **Range:** Self  
> **Components:** V, S  
> **Duration:** 1 round  
>
> An imperceptible barrier of magical force protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from Magic Missile.
>
