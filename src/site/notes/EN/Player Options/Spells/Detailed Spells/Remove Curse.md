> [!spell|no-t]
> # Remove Curse
>
> *Level 3 Abjuration ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Cleric/Cleric\|Cleric]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Paladin/Paladin\|Paladin]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Warlock/Warlock\|Warlock]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])*
>
>
> **Casting Time:** Action  
> **Range:** Touch  
> **Components:** V, S  
> **Duration:** Instantaneous  
>
> At your touch, all curses affecting one creature or object end. If the object is a cursed magic item, its curse remains, but the spell breaks its owner’s Attunement to the object so it can be removed or discarded.
>
