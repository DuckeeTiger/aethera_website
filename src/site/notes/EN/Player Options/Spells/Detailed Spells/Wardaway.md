> # Wardaway
>
> *Level 1 Abjuration ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Bard/Bard\|Bard]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Cleric/Cleric\|Cleric]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Paladin/Paladin\|Paladin]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Wizard/Wizard\|Wizard]])*
>
> **Casting Time:** Action  
> **Range:** 60 feet  
> **Components:** V, S, M (a miniature clay hand)  
> **Duration:** Instantaneous  
>
> You hurl a disorienting magical force toward one creature within range.
>
> The target makes a Constitution saving throw; Constructs and Undead automatically succeed on this save.
>
> On a failed save, the target:
>
> - takes 2d4 Force damage
> - has its Speed halved until the start of your next turn
> - and on its next turn, it can take only an action or a Bonus Action (but not both)
>
> On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage only.
>
> ***Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.*** The damage increases by 2d4 for every spell slot level above 1.
>
