> # Spare the Dying
>
> *Necromancy Cantrip ([[EN/Player Options/Classes/Artificer/Artificer\|Artificer]] [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Cleric/Cleric\|Cleric]], [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Druid/Druid\|Druid]])*

> **Range:** 15 feet  
> **Components:** V, S

> ***Cantrip Upgrade.*** The range doubles when you reach levels 5 (30 feet), 11 (60 feet), and 17 (120 feet).
