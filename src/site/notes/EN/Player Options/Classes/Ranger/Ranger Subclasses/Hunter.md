> [!class-recite|ranger]
> *Protect Nature and People from Destruction*
>
> Hunters stalk dangerous prey across the wilderness and beyond, using their skills and instincts to safeguard both civilization and the natural world.
>
> Whether facing marauding monsters, rampaging beasts, or supernatural threats, a Hunter learns how to adapt their tactics to overcome any foe.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Hunter's Lore
> ##### Level 3 Hunter Feature
>
> You can call on the forces of nature to reveal the strengths and weaknesses of your prey.
>
> While a creature is marked by your [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Hunter's Mark\|Hunter's Mark]], you know whether that creature has any Immunities, Resistances, or Vulnerabilities.
>
> If it has any, you know exactly what they are.

> [!class-divider|ranger small]
> ---

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Hunter's Prey
> ##### Level 3 Hunter Feature
>
> You gain one of the following feature options of your choice.
>
> Whenever you finish a Short or Long Rest, you can replace the chosen option with the other one.
>
> - **Colossus Slayer.**
>
>   Your tenacity can wear down even the most resilient foes.
>
>   Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a weapon and that creature is missing any of its Hit Points, the attack deals an extra **1d8 damage**.
>
> - **Horde Breaker.**
>
>   Once on each of your turns when you make an attack with a weapon, you can make another attack with the same weapon against a different creature.
>
>   The second target must:
>
>   - Be within 5 feet of the original target.
>   - Be within the weapon's range.
>   - Not have been attacked by you this turn.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Defensive Tactics
> ##### Level 7 Hunter Feature
>
> You gain one of the following feature options of your choice.
>
> Whenever you finish a Short or Long Rest, you can replace the chosen option with the other one.
>
> - **Escape the Horde.**
>
>   Opportunity Attacks against you have Disadvantage.
>
> - **Multiattack Defense.**
>
>   When a creature hits you with an attack roll, that creature has Disadvantage on all other attack rolls it makes against you until the end of the current turn.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 11

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Superior Hunter's Prey
> ##### Level 11 Hunter Feature
>
> Once per turn when you deal damage to a creature marked by your [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Hunter's Mark\|Hunter's Mark]], you can also deal the spell's extra damage to a different creature you can see within 30 feet of the original target.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Superior Hunter's Defense
> ##### Level 15 Hunter Feature
>
> When you take damage, you can use your Reaction to gain Resistance to that damage type and any other damage of the same type until the end of the current turn.
