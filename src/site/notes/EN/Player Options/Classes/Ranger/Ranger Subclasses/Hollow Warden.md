> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Wrath of the Wild
> ##### Level 3 Hollow Warden Feature
>
> You draw power from the strange and ancient horrors of the wild.
>
> Bloody antlers, rotting fangs, twisted shadows, or other unsettling manifestations emerge around you.
>
> As a Bonus Action, you can expend a use of Favored Enemy to transform into a ghastly form.
>
> The transformation lasts for 1 minute or until you gain the Incapacitated condition, die, or end it early (no action required).
>
> While transformed, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Ancient Armor.** You gain a +1 bonus to AC. This bonus increases to +2 when you reach Ranger level 11.
>
> - **Prowling Retribution.** Immediately after a creature you can see within 5 feet of yourself deals damage to you or one of your allies, you can make an Opportunity Attack against that creature.
>
> - **Unnerving Aura.** When you transform and at the start of each of your turns thereafter, each creature of your choice in a 10-foot Emanation originating from you must make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC. On a failed save, the creature has the [[EN/Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Frightened\|Frightened]] condition until the start of your next turn.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Hungering Might
> ##### Level 7 Hollow Warden Feature
>
> You gain a bonus to Constitution saving throws equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of +1).
>
> In addition, once per turn while transformed by Wrath of the Wild, when you hit a creature with an attack roll, you regain Hit Points equal to 1d10 plus your Wisdom modifier if you are [[Bloodied\|Bloodied]] when the attack hits.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 11

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Rot and Violence
> ##### Level 11 Hollow Warden Feature
>
> Your devotion to ancient wild horrors further transforms your body and spirit.
>
> While transformed by Wrath of the Wild, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Menacing Aura.** When a creature fails its saving throw against your Unnerving Aura, it also can't regain Hit Points or take Reactions until the start of your next turn.
>
> - **Strangling Roots.** When you hit a creature with a weapon attack, you can activate the Sap or Slow Weapon Mastery property in addition to another mastery property you are using with that weapon.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Ancient Might
> ##### Level 15 Hollow Warden Feature
>
> You become fully suffused with the terrible power of the ancient wilds.
>
> You gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Ominous Strikes.** When you hit a creature that has the [[EN/Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Frightened\|Frightened]] condition with an attack roll, the attack deals extra damage equal to your Wisdom modifier.
>
> - **Persistent Wrath.** If you are reduced to 0 Hit Points but not killed outright while transformed by Wrath of the Wild, your Hit Points instead become equal to twice your Ranger level.
>
>   Once you use this benefit, you can't do so again until you finish a Long Rest.
>
>   You can also restore a use by expending a level 4+ spell slot (no action required).
>
> - **Timeless.** You gain Immunity to the [[EN/Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Exhaustion\|Exhaustion]] condition.
