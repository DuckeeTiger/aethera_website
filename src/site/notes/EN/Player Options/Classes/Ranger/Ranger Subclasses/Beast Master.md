> [!class-recite|ranger]
> *Bond with a Primal Beast*
>
> A Beast Master forms a mystical bond with a special animal, drawing on primal magic and a deep connection to the natural world.
>
> Whether prowling through dense forests, soaring across open skies, or hunting beneath crashing waves, a Beast Master's companion serves as a loyal ally and trusted friend.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Primal Companion
> ##### Level 3 Beast Master Feature
>
> You magically summon a primal beast that draws strength from your bond with nature.
>
> Choose one of the following stat blocks:
>
> - **Beast of the Land**
> - **Beast of the Sea**
> - **Beast of the Sky**
>
> You also determine the beast's appearance, choosing an appropriate animal form for the selected stat block.
>
> The beast is Friendly to you and your allies and obeys your commands.
>
> It vanishes if you die.
>
> **The Beast in Combat.**
>
> In combat, the beast acts during your turn.
>
> It can move and use its Reaction on its own, but the only action it takes is Dodge unless you:
>
> - Use a Bonus Action to command it to take an action from its stat block or another action.
> - Sacrifice one of your attacks when taking the Attack action to command it to take the Beast's Strike action.
>
> If you have the Incapacitated condition, the beast acts independently and is no longer limited to Dodge.
>
> **Restoring or Replacing the Beast.**
>
> If the beast died within the last hour, you can take a Magic action, touch it, and expend a spell slot.
>
> After 1 minute, the beast returns to life with all Hit Points restored.
>
> Whenever you finish a Long Rest, you may summon a different primal beast in an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you.
>
> If you already have a beast from this feature, the old one vanishes when the new one appears.

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Exceptional Training
> ##### Level 7 Beast Master Feature
>
> When you take a Bonus Action to command your Primal Companion to take an action, you can also command it to take one of the following actions using its Bonus Action:
>
> - Dash
> - Disengage
> - Dodge
> - Help
>
> In addition, whenever the beast hits with an attack roll and deals damage, it can deal either:
>
> - Its normal damage type, or
> - Force damage

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 11

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Bestial Fury
> ##### Level 11 Beast Master Feature
>
> When you command your Primal Companion to take the Beast's Strike action, it can make two Beast's Strike attacks instead of one.
>
> In addition, the first time each turn the beast hits a creature affected by your [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Hunter's Mark\|Hunter's Mark]], it deals extra Force damage equal to the bonus damage granted by that spell.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Share Spells
> ##### Level 15 Beast Master Feature
>
> When you cast a spell targeting yourself, you can also affect your Primal Companion with that spell if the beast is within 30 feet of you.
