> [!class-recite|ranger]
> *Wield Fey Mirth and Fury*
>
> A fey mystique surrounds you, thanks to the boon of an archfey or a location in the Feywild that transformed you.
>
> However you gained fey magic, you are now a Fey Wanderer. Your joyful laughter brightens the hearts of the downtrodden, while your martial prowess strikes terror into your foes.
>
> Great is the mirth of the fey, and dreadful is their fury.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Dreadful Strikes
> ##### Level 3 Fey Wanderer Feature
>
> You can augment your weapon strikes with mind-scarring magic drawn from the murky hollows of the Feywild.
>
> When you hit a creature with a weapon, you can deal an extra **1d4 Psychic damage** to the target.
>
> A creature can take this extra damage only once per turn.
>
> The extra damage increases to **1d6** when you reach Ranger level 11.

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Otherworldly Glamour
> ##### Level 3 Fey Wanderer Feature
>
> Whenever you make a Charisma check, you gain a bonus equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum bonus of +1).
>
> In addition, you gain proficiency in one of the following skills of your choice:
>
> - Deception
> - Performance
> - Persuasion

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Beguiling Twist
> ##### Level 7 Fey Wanderer Feature
>
> The magic of the Feywild guards your mind.
>
> You have Advantage on saving throws made to avoid or end the Charmed or Frightened condition.
>
> In addition, whenever you or a creature you can see within 120 feet succeeds on a saving throw to avoid or end either of those conditions, you can use your Reaction to force a different creature you can see within 120 feet of yourself to make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC.
>
> On a failed save, the target is either Charmed or Frightened (your choice) for 1 minute.
>
> The target repeats the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 11

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Fey Reinforcements
> ##### Level 11 Fey Wanderer Feature
>
> You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Summon Fey\|Summon Fey]] without requiring a Material component.
>
> In addition, you can cast the spell once without expending a spell slot.
>
> You regain the ability to do so when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> Whenever you cast the spell, you can modify it so that it doesn't require Concentration.
>
> If you do so, its duration becomes 1 minute for that casting.

> [!class-level|ranger]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|ranger]
> #### Misty Wanderer
> ##### Level 15 Fey Wanderer Feature
>
> You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Misty Step\|Misty Step]] without expending a spell slot.
>
> You can do so a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> In addition, whenever you cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Misty Step\|Misty Step]], you can bring along one willing creature you can see within 5 feet of yourself.
>
> That creature teleports to an unoccupied space of your choice within 5 feet of your destination.
