> [!class-recite|paladin]
> *Punish Evildoers at Any Cost*
>
> The Oath of Vengeance is a solemn commitment to punish those who have committed grievously evil acts.
>
> When evil armies slaughter helpless villagers, when a tyrant defies the will of the gods, when a thieves’ guild grows too violent, or when a dragon rampages through the countryside, Paladins arise and swear an Oath of Vengeance to set right what has gone wrong.
>
> These Paladins commonly follow these tenets:
>
> - Show the wicked no mercy.
> - Fight injustice and its causes.
> - Aid those harmed by injustice.

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Vow of Enmity
> ##### Level 3 Oath of Vengeance Feature
>
> When you take the Attack action, you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity to utter a vow of enmity against a creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself.
>
> You have Advantage on attack rolls against that creature for 1 minute or until you use this feature again.
>
> If the creature drops to 0 Hit Points before the vow ends, you can transfer the vow to a different creature within 30 feet of yourself (no action required).

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Relentless Avenger
> ##### Level 7 Oath of Vengeance Feature
>
> Your supernatural focus helps you close off a foe’s retreat.
>
> When you hit a creature with an Opportunity Attack, you can reduce that creature’s Speed to 0 until the end of the current turn.
>
> You can then move up to half your Speed as part of the same Reaction.
>
> This movement doesn’t provoke Opportunity Attacks.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Soul of Vengeance
> ##### Level 15 Oath of Vengeance Feature
>
> Immediately after a creature under the effect of your Vow of Enmity hits or misses with an attack roll, you can take a Reaction to make a melee attack against that creature if it is within range.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 20

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Avenging Angel
> ##### Level 20 Oath of Vengeance Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you gain the following benefits for 10 minutes or until you end them (no action required).
>
> Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
>
> You can also restore its use by expending a level 5 spell slot (no action required).
>
> While this feature is active, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Flight.** You sprout spectral wings, gain a Fly Speed of 60 feet, and can hover.
>
> - **Frightful Aura.** Whenever an enemy starts its turn within your Aura of Protection, it must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or gain the Frightened condition for 1 minute or until it takes any damage.
>
>   Attack rolls against a creature Frightened by this feature have Advantage.
