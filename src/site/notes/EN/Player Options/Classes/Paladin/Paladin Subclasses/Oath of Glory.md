> [!class-recite|paladin]
> *Strive for the Heights of Heroism*
>
> Paladins who take the Oath of Glory believe they and their companions are destined to achieve glory through deeds of heroism.
>
> They train diligently and encourage their companions so they are ready when destiny calls.
>
> Glory Paladins commonly follow these tenets:
>
> - Endeavor to be known by your deeds.
> - Face hardships with courage.
> - Inspire others to strive for glory.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Inspiring Smite
> ##### Level 3 Oath of Glory Feature
>
> Immediately after you cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Divine Smite\|Divine Smite]], you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity and distribute Temporary Hit Points to creatures of your choice within 30 feet of yourself, including yourself.
>
> The total number of Temporary Hit Points equals 2d8 plus your Paladin level.
>
> Divide those Temporary Hit Points among the chosen creatures however you like.

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Peerless Athlete
> ##### Level 3 Oath of Glory Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity to augment your athleticism.
>
> For 1 hour, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - Advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks.
> - Advantage on Dexterity (Acrobatics) checks.
> - Your Long Jump and High Jump distances increase by 10 feet.
>
> This additional jump distance costs movement as normal.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Aura of Alacrity
> ##### Level 7 Oath of Glory Feature
>
> Your Speed increases by 10 feet.
>
> In addition, whenever an ally enters your Aura of Protection for the first time on a turn or starts its turn there, that ally’s Speed increases by 10 feet until the end of its next turn.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Glorious Defense
> ##### Level 15 Oath of Glory Feature
>
> You can turn defense into a sudden strike.
>
> When you or another creature you can see within 10 feet of yourself is hit by an attack roll, you can take a Reaction to grant a bonus to the target’s AC against that attack.
>
> The bonus equals your Charisma modifier (minimum of +1), potentially causing the attack to miss.
>
> If the attack misses, you can immediately make one attack with a weapon against the attacker as part of the same Reaction, provided the attacker is within your weapon’s range.
>
> You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 20

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Living Legend
> ##### Level 20 Oath of Glory Feature
>
> You can empower yourself with the legends, whether true or exaggerated, of your heroic deeds.
>
> As a Bonus Action, you gain the following benefits for 10 minutes:
>
> - **Charismatic.** You have Advantage on all Charisma checks.
>
> - **Saving Throw Reroll.** If you fail a saving throw, you can take a Reaction to reroll it. You must use the new roll.
>
> - **Unerring Strike.** Once on each of your turns, when you miss with an attack roll made using a weapon, you can cause that attack to hit instead.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
>
> You can also restore its use by expending a level 5 spell slot (no action required).
