> [!class-recite|paladin]
> *Brandish the Elemental Splendor of Genies*
>
> Paladins sworn to the Oath of the Noble Genies revere the forces of the Elemental Planes.
>
> Through taking this oath, Paladins draw power from the four great genie kinds:
>
> - **Dao**, masters of earth
> - **Djinn**, masters of air
> - **Efreet**, masters of fire
> - **Marids**, masters of water
>
> Many who swear this oath hail from Calimshan, a land rich with genie influence.
>
> These paladins share the following tenets:
>
> - Sow the seeds of creation amid the ashes of destruction.
> - Lead with splendor and grace.
> - Respect the elements, and fear their wrath.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Elemental Smite
> ##### Level 3 Oath of the Noble Genies Feature
>
> Immediately after you cast Divine Smite, you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity and invoke one of the following effects:
>
> **Dao's Crush.**
>
> Earth rises around the target of your Divine Smite.
>
> The target gains the Grappled condition (escape DC equals your spell save DC). While Grappled in this way, the target also has the Restrained condition.
>
> **Djinni's Escape.**
>
> You teleport to an unoccupied space you can see within 30 feet.
>
> Until the end of your next turn, you gain:
>
> - Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage
> - Immunity to the Grappled condition
> - Immunity to the Prone condition
> - Immunity to the Restrained condition
>
> **Efreeti's Fury.**
>
> The target of your Divine Smite takes an extra 2d4 Fire damage.
>
> Fire then leaps to another creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself. That creature also takes 2d4 Fire damage.
>
> **Marid's Surge.**
>
> The target of your Divine Smite and each creature of your choice in a 10-foot Emanation originating from you must make a Strength saving throw against your spell save DC.
>
> On a failed save, a creature:
>
> - Is pushed 15 feet directly away from you.
> - Gains the Prone condition.

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Genie's Splendor
> ##### Level 3 Oath of the Noble Genies Feature
>
> While you aren't wearing armor, your base Armor Class equals:
>
> **10 + your Dexterity modifier + your Charisma modifier**
>
> You can use a Shield and still gain this benefit.
>
> In addition, you gain proficiency in one of the following skills of your choice:
>
> - Acrobatics
> - Intimidation
> - Performance
> - Persuasion

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Aura of Elemental Shielding
> ##### Level 7 Oath of the Noble Genies Feature
>
> Choose one of the following damage types:
>
> - Acid
> - Cold
> - Fire
> - Lightning
> - Thunder
>
> You and your allies have Resistance to the chosen damage type while within your Aura of Protection.
>
> At the start of each of your turns, you can change the chosen damage type to another option from the list (no action required).

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Elemental Rebuke
> ##### Level 15 Oath of the Noble Genies Feature
>
> When you are hit by an attack roll, you can take a Reaction to halve the attack's damage against you (round down).
>
> The attacker must then make a Dexterity saving throw against your spell save DC.
>
> On a failed save, the attacker takes:
>
> **2d10 + your Charisma modifier**
>
> damage of a type of your choice:
>
> - Acid
> - Cold
> - Fire
> - Lightning
> - Thunder
>
> On a successful save, the attacker takes half as much damage.
>
> You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once), and regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 20

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Noble Scion
> ##### Level 20 Oath of the Noble Genies Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you gain the following benefits for 10 minutes or until you end them (no action required).
>
> Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
>
> You can also restore your use by expending a level 5 spell slot (no action required).
>
> - **Flight.** You gain a Fly Speed of 60 feet and can hover.
>
> - **Minor Wish.** When you or an ally within your Aura of Protection fails a D20 Test, you can take a Reaction to cause that creature to succeed instead.
