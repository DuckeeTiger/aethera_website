> [!class-recite|paladin]
> *Preserve Life and Light in the World*
>
> The Oath of the Ancients is as old as the first elves.
>
> Paladins who swear this oath cherish the light and love the beautiful, life-giving things of the world more than any principles of honor, courage, or justice.
>
> They often adorn their armor and clothing with images of growing things such as leaves, antlers, or flowers to reflect their commitment to preserving life and light.
>
> Ancients Paladins commonly follow these tenets:
>
> - Kindle the light of hope.
> - Shelter life.
> - Delight in art and laughter.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Nature’s Wrath
> ##### Level 3 Oath of the Ancients Feature
>
> As a Magic action, you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity to conjure spectral vines around nearby creatures.
>
> Each creature of your choice that you can see within 15 feet of yourself must succeed on a Strength saving throw or gain the Restrained condition for 1 minute.
>
> A Restrained creature repeats the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Aura of Warding
> ##### Level 7 Oath of the Ancients Feature
>
> Ancient magic lies heavily upon you, forming an eldritch ward that blunts energy from beyond the Material Plane.
>
> You and your allies have Resistance to Necrotic, Psychic, and Radiant damage while within your Aura of Protection.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Undying Sentinel
> ##### Level 15 Oath of the Ancients Feature
>
> When you are reduced to 0 Hit Points and not killed outright, you can instead drop to 1 Hit Point and immediately regain Hit Points equal to three times your Paladin level.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
>
> In addition, you can’t be aged magically, and you cease visibly aging.

> [!class-level|paladin]
> Level 20

> [!class-feature|paladin]
> #### Elder Champion
> ##### Level 20 Oath of the Ancients Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you can imbue your Aura of Protection with primal power for 1 minute or until you end the effect (no action required).
>
> Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
>
> You can also restore its use by expending a level 5 spell slot (no action required).
>
> While the effect is active, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Diminish Defiance.** Enemies within your aura have Disadvantage on saving throws against your spells and Channel Divinity options.
>
> - **Regeneration.** At the start of each of your turns, you regain 10 Hit Points.
>
> - **Swift Spells.** Whenever you cast a spell that has a casting time of an action, you can cast it using a Bonus Action instead.
