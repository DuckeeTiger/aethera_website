> [!class-recite|fighter]
> *Augment Physical Might with Psionic Power*
>
> Psi Warriors awaken the power of their minds to augment their physical might.
>
> They harness this psionic power to infuse their weapon strikes, lash out with telekinetic energy, and create barriers of mental force.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Psionic Power
> ##### Level 3 Psi Warrior Feature
>
> You harbor a wellspring of psionic energy within yourself.
>
> It is represented by your Psionic Energy Dice, which fuel powers you have from this subclass.
>
> The Psi Warrior Energy Dice table shows the die size and number of these dice you have when you reach certain Fighter levels.
>
>> [!class-table|fighter]
>> | Fighter Level | Die Size | Number |
>> | ----- | ----- | ----- |
>> | 3 | d6 | 4 |
>> | 5 | d8 | 6 |
>> | 9 | d8 | 8 |
>> | 11 | d10 | 8 |
>> | 13 | d10 | 10 |
>> | 17 | d12 | 12 |
>
> Any feature in this subclass that uses a Psionic Energy Die uses only the dice from this subclass.
>
> Some powers expend a Psionic Energy Die as part of their use.
>
> You regain one expended Psionic Energy Die when you finish a Short Rest, and regain all expended dice when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> **Protective Field**
>
> When you or another creature you can see within 30 feet takes damage, you can take a Reaction to expend one Psionic Energy Die.
>
> Roll the die and reduce the damage by the number rolled + your Intelligence modifier (minimum reduction of 1).
>
> **Psionic Strike**
>
> Once on each of your turns, immediately after you hit a target within 30 feet of yourself with a weapon attack, you can expend one Psionic Energy Die.
>
> Roll the die and deal Force damage equal to the result + your Intelligence modifier.
>
> **Telekinetic Movement**
>
> As a Magic action, choose one target you can see within 30 feet.
>
> The target must be either:
>
> - A loose object that is Large or smaller
> - A willing creature other than yourself
>
> You transport the target up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see.
>
> If the target is a Tiny object, you can instead move it to or from your hand.
>
> Once you use this action, you can’t do so again until you finish a Short or Long Rest unless you expend a Psionic Energy Die to restore its use.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Telekinetic Adept
> ##### Level 7 Psi Warrior Feature
>
> You have mastered new ways to use your telekinetic abilities.
>
> **Psi-Powered Leap**
>
> As a Bonus Action, you gain a Fly Speed equal to twice your Speed until the end of the current turn.
>
> Once you use this Bonus Action, you can’t do so again until you finish a Short or Long Rest unless you expend a Psionic Energy Die to restore its use.
>
> **Telekinetic Thrust**
>
> When you deal damage with your Psionic Strike, you can force the target to make a Strength saving throw.
>
> The save DC equals 8 + your Intelligence modifier + your Proficiency Bonus.
>
> On a failed save, you can either:
>
> - Give the target the Prone condition
> - Move the target up to 10 feet horizontally

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Guarded Mind
> ##### Level 10 Psi Warrior Feature
>
> You have Resistance to Psychic damage.
>
> In addition, if you start your turn with the Charmed or Frightened condition, you can expend a Psionic Energy Die and end every effect on yourself causing those conditions.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Bulwark of Force
> ##### Level 15 Psi Warrior Feature
>
> You can shield yourself and others with telekinetic force.
>
> As a Bonus Action, choose a number of creatures within 30 feet of yourself, including yourself, up to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of one creature).
>
> Each chosen creature gains Half Cover for 1 minute or until you have the Incapacitated condition.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can’t do so again until you finish a Long Rest unless you expend a Psionic Energy Die to restore its use.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 18

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Telekinetic Master
> ##### Level 18 Psi Warrior Feature
>
> You always have the [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Telekinesis\|Telekinesis]] spell prepared.
>
> With this feature, you can cast it without a spell slot or material components, and Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for it.
>
> On each of your turns while you maintain Concentration on the spell, including the turn you cast it, you can make one weapon attack as a Bonus Action.
>
> Once you cast the spell with this feature, you can’t do so again until you finish a Long Rest unless you expend a Psionic Energy Die to restore its use.
