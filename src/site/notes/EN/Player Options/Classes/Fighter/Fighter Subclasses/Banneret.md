> [!class-recite|fighter]
> *Rally Fellow Heroes with Inspiring Leadership*
>
> Bannerets are paragons of valor and leadership who protect the innocent and rally fellow adventurers to the causes of justice and freedom.
>
> Many are knights serving in Cormyr, the Silver Marches, Damara, Chessenta, and other lands across Faerûn.
>
> A lone Banneret is a skilled warrior, but when leading companions into battle, they can transform even a poorly equipped militia into a formidable fighting force.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Knightly Envoy
> ##### Level 3 Banneret Feature
>
> You know how to conduct yourself with grace as a noble ambassador.
>
> You gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Comprehension.** You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Comprehend Languages\|Comprehend Languages]] as a Ritual. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the spell.
>
> - **Polyglot.** You learn one language from the language tables in the Player's Handbook or chapter 2 of this book.
>
>   Whenever you finish a Long Rest, you can replace that language with another language you have heard, seen signed, or read during the previous 24 hours.
>
> - **Well Spoken.** You gain proficiency in one of the following skills of your choice:
>
>   - Insight
>   - Intimidation
>   - Persuasion
>   - Performance

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Group Recovery
> ##### Level 3 Banneret Feature
>
> When you use your Second Wind to regain Hit Points, you can choose allies within a 30-foot Emanation originating from yourself, up to a number of allies equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of one ally).
>
> Each chosen ally regains Hit Points equal to 1d4 plus your Fighter level.
>
> Once you use this ability, you can't do so again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Team Tactics
> ##### Level 7 Banneret Feature
>
> When you use Group Recovery, each chosen ally gains Advantage on D20 Tests until the start of your next turn.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Rallying Surge
> ##### Level 10 Banneret Feature
>
> When you use your Action Surge, you can choose allies within a 30-foot Emanation originating from yourself, up to a number of allies equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of one ally).
>
> Each chosen ally can immediately take a Reaction to use one of the following options:
>
> - **Attack.** Make one attack with a weapon or an Unarmed Strike.
>
> - **Move.** Move up to half your Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Shared Resilience
> ##### Level 15 Banneret Feature
>
> When an ally you can see within 60 feet of yourself fails a saving throw, you can take a Reaction and expend one use of your Indomitable feature.
>
> The ally immediately rerolls the saving throw and gains a bonus to the roll equal to your Fighter level.
>
> The ally must use the new roll.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 18

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Inspiring Commander
> ##### Level 18 Banneret Feature
>
> You gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Bolstered Rally.** The area of effect of both Group Recovery and Rallying Surge increases to a 60-foot Emanation.
>
> - **Unshakable Bravery.** You have Immunity to the Charmed and Frightened conditions.
