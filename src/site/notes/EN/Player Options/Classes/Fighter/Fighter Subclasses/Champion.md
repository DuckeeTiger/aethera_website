> [!class-recite|fighter]
> *Pursue Physical Excellence in Combat*
>
> A Champion focuses on the development of martial prowess in a relentless pursuit of victory.
>
> Champions combine rigorous training with physical excellence to deal devastating blows, withstand peril, and garner glory.
>
> Whether in athletic contests or bloody battle, Champions strive for the crown of the victor.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Improved Critical
> ##### Level 3 Champion Feature
>
> Your attack rolls with weapons and Unarmed Strikes can score a Critical Hit on a roll of 19 or 20 on the d20.

> [!class-divider|fighter small]
> ---

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Remarkable Athlete
> ##### Level 3 Champion Feature
>
> Thanks to your athleticism, you have Advantage on Initiative rolls and Strength (Athletics) checks.
>
> In addition, immediately after you score a Critical Hit, you can move up to half your Speed without provoking Opportunity Attacks.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 7

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Additional Fighting Style
> ##### Level 7 Champion Feature
>
> You gain another Fighting Style feat of your choice.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Heroic Warrior
> ##### Level 10 Champion Feature
>
> The thrill of battle drives you toward victory.
>
> During combat, you can give yourself Heroic Inspiration whenever you start your turn without it.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 15

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Superior Critical
> ##### Level 15 Champion Feature
>
> Your attack rolls with weapons and Unarmed Strikes can now score a Critical Hit on a roll of 18–20 on the d20.

> [!class-level|fighter]
> Level 18

> [!class-feature|fighter]
> #### Survivor
> ##### Level 18 Champion Feature
>
> You attain the pinnacle of resilience in battle, gaining the following benefits.
>
> **Defy Death.**
>
> You have Advantage on Death Saving Throws.
>
> Moreover, when you roll 18–20 on a Death Saving Throw, you gain the benefit of rolling a 20 on it.
>
> **Heroic Rally.**
>
> At the start of each of your turns, you regain Hit Points equal to 5 + your Constitution modifier if you are Bloodied and have at least 1 Hit Point.
