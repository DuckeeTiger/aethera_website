> [!class-recite|druid]
> *Adopt Animal Forms to Guard the Wilds*
>
> Druids of the Circle of the Moon draw on lunar magic to transform themselves. Their order gathers under the moon to share news and perform rituals.
>
> Changeable as the moon, a Druid of this circle might prowl as a great cat one night, soar over the treetops as an eagle the next day, and then crash through undergrowth as a bear to drive off a trespassing monster.
>
> The wild is in the Druid’s blood.

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Circle Forms
> ##### Level 3 Circle of the Moon Feature
>
> You can channel lunar magic when you assume a Wild Shape form, granting the following benefits:
>
> - **Challenge Rating.** The maximum Challenge Rating for the form equals your Druid level divided by 3 (round down).
> - **Armor Class.** Until you leave the form, your AC equals 13 plus your Wisdom modifier if that total is higher than the Beast’s AC.
> - **Temporary Hit Points.** You gain a number of Temporary Hit Points equal to three times your Druid level.

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Improved Circle Forms
> ##### Level 6 Circle of the Moon Feature
>
> While in a Wild Shape form, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Lunar Radiance.** Each of your attacks in a Wild Shape form can deal either their normal damage type or Radiant damage. You choose each time you hit.
> - **Increased Toughness.** You can add your Wisdom modifier to Constitution saving throws.

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Moonlight Step
> ##### Level 10 Circle of the Moon Feature
>
> You magically transport yourself, reappearing amid a burst of moonlight.
>
> As a Bonus Action, you teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see.
>
> You then gain Advantage on the next attack roll you make before the end of the current turn.
>
> You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> You can also restore a use by expending a level 2+ spell slot (no action required).

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Lunar Form
> ##### Level 14 Circle of the Moon Feature
>
> The power of the moon suffuses you, granting the following benefits:
>
> - **Improved Lunar Radiance.** Once per turn, when you hit a creature with a Wild Shape form's attack, you can deal an extra 2d10 Radiant damage.
> - **Shared Moonlight.** Whenever you use Moonlight Step, you can also teleport one willing creature. The creature must be within 10 feet of you, and it teleports to an unoccupied space you can see within 10 feet of your destination.
