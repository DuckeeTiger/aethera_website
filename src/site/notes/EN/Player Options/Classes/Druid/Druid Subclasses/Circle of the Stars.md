> [!class-recite|druid]
> *Harness Secrets Hidden in Constellations*
>
> The Circle of the Stars has tracked heavenly patterns since time immemorial, discovering secrets hidden amid the constellations.
>
> By understanding these secrets, the Druids of this circle seek to harness the powers of the cosmos.

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Star Map
> ##### Level 3 Circle of the Stars Feature
>
> You’ve created a star chart as part of your heavenly studies. It is a Tiny object, and you can use it as a Spellcasting Focus for your Druid spells.
>
> You determine its form by rolling on the Star Map table or by choosing one.
>
> While holding the map, you always have [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Guidance\|Guidance]] and [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Guiding Bolt\|Guiding Bolt]] prepared, and you can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Guiding Bolt\|Guiding Bolt]] without expending a spell slot.
>
> You can cast it in this way a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> If you lose the map, you can perform a 1-hour ceremony to magically create a replacement. This ceremony can be performed during a Short or Long Rest and destroys the previous map.
>

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Starry Form
> ##### Level 3 Circle of the Stars Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you can expend a use of your Wild Shape feature to take on a starry form rather than shape-shifting.
>
> While in your Starry Form, you retain your game statistics, but your body becomes luminous. Your joints glimmer like stars, and glowing lines connect them as on a star chart.
>
> This form sheds Bright Light in a 10-foot radius and Dim Light for an additional 10 feet.
>
> The form lasts for 10 minutes. It ends early if you dismiss it (no action required), gain the Incapacitated condition, or use this feature again.
>
> Whenever you assume your Starry Form, choose one of the following constellations:
>
> - **Archer.** A constellation of an archer appears on you. When you activate this form and as a Bonus Action on your subsequent turns, you can make a ranged spell attack against a creature within 60 feet. On a hit, the target takes Radiant damage equal to 1d8 plus your Wisdom modifier.
>
> - **Chalice.** A constellation of a life-giving goblet appears on you. Whenever you cast a spell using a spell slot that restores Hit Points to a creature, you or another creature within 30 feet regains Hit Points equal to 1d8 plus your Wisdom modifier.
>
> - **Dragon.** A constellation of a wise dragon appears on you. When you make an Intelligence check, Wisdom check, or Constitution saving throw to maintain Concentration, you can treat a d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Cosmic Omen
> ##### Level 6 Circle of the Stars Feature
>
> Whenever you finish a Long Rest, you can consult your Star Map for omens and roll a die.
>
> Until you finish your next Long Rest, you gain access to one of the following Reactions:
>
> - **Weal (Even).** Whenever a creature you can see within 30 feet is about to make a D20 Test, you can take a Reaction to roll 1d6 and add the result to the total.
>
> - **Woe (Odd).** Whenever a creature you can see within 30 feet is about to make a D20 Test, you can take a Reaction to roll 1d6 and subtract the result from the total.
>
> You can use this Reaction a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Twinkling Constellations
> ##### Level 10 Circle of the Stars Feature
>
> The constellations of your Starry Form improve.
>
> - The Archer and Chalice benefits increase from 1d8 to 2d8.
> - While Dragon is active, you gain a Fly Speed of 20 feet and can hover.
>
> In addition, at the start of each of your turns while in your Starry Form, you can change which constellation glimmers on your body.

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Full of Stars
> ##### Level 14 Circle of the Stars Feature
>
> While in your Starry Form, you become partially incorporeal.
>
> You gain Resistance to Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing damage.
