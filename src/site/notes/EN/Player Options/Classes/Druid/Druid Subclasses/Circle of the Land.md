> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Land's Aid
> ##### Level 3 Circle of the Land Feature
>
> As a Magic action, you can expend a use of your Wild Shape and choose a point within 60 feet of yourself.
>
> Vitality-giving flowers and life-draining thorns appear for a moment in a 10-foot-radius Sphere centered on that point.
>
> Each creature of your choice in the Sphere must make a Constitution saving throw against your spell save DC.
>
> On a failed save, a creature takes 2d6 Necrotic damage.
>
> On a successful save, it takes half as much damage.
>
> One creature of your choice in the area regains 2d6 Hit Points.
>
> The damage and healing increase when you reach higher Druid levels:
>
> - Level 10: 3d6
> - Level 14: 4d6

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Natural Recovery
> ##### Level 6 Circle of the Land Feature
>
> You can cast one of the level 1+ spells prepared through your Circle of the Land Spells feature without expending a spell slot.
>
> Once you do so, you must finish a Long Rest before using this benefit again.
>
> In addition, when you finish a Short Rest, you can recover expended spell slots.
>
> The recovered slots can have a combined level equal to or less than half your Druid level (rounded up), and none of the recovered slots can be level 6 or higher.
>
> For example, a level 6 Druid can recover up to three levels worth of spell slots.
>
> Once you recover spell slots using this feature, you can’t do so again until you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Nature's Ward
> ##### Level 10 Circle of the Land Feature
>
> You are immune to the Poisoned condition.
>
> In addition, you gain Resistance to a damage type associated with your current land choice.
>

> [!class-level|druid]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|druid]
> #### Nature's Sanctuary
> ##### Level 14 Circle of the Land Feature
>
> As a Magic action, you can expend a use of your Wild Shape and cause spectral trees and vines to appear in a 15-foot Cube on the ground within 120 feet of yourself.
>
> The area lasts for 1 minute or until you have the Incapacitated condition or die.
>
> You and your allies have Half Cover while within the area.
>
> In addition, your allies gain the Resistance provided by your Nature's Ward feature while inside the area.
>
> As a Bonus Action, you can move the Cube up to 60 feet to another location on the ground within 120 feet of yourself.
