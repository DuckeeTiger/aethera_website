> [!class-recite|monk]
> *Wield Strikes and Bursts of Elemental Power*
>
> Warriors of the Elements tap into the power of the Elemental Planes.
>
> Harnessing their supernatural focus, these Monks momentarily tame the energy of the Elemental Chaos to empower themselves in and out of battle.

> [!class-level|monk]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|monk]
> #### Elemental Attunement
> ##### Level 3 Warrior of the Elements Feature
>
> At the start of your turn, you can expend 1 Focus Point to imbue yourself with elemental energy.
>
> The energy lasts for 10 minutes or until you gain the Incapacitated condition.
>
> While this feature is active, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Reach.** When you make an Unarmed Strike, your reach increases by 10 feet as elemental energy extends from you.
>
> - **Elemental Strikes.** Whenever you hit with an Unarmed Strike, you can cause it to deal Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage instead of its normal damage type.
>
>   When you deal one of these damage types, you can force the target to make a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, you can move the target up to 10 feet toward or away from you.

> [!class-divider|monk small]

> [!class-feature|monk]
> #### Manipulate Elements
> ##### Level 3 Warrior of the Elements Feature
>
> You know the [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Elementalism\|Elementalism]] spell.
>
> Wisdom is your spellcasting ability for it.

> [!class-level|monk]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|monk]
> #### Elemental Burst
> ##### Level 6 Warrior of the Elements Feature
>
> As a Magic action, you can expend 2 Focus Points to cause elemental energy to erupt in a 20-foot-radius Sphere centered on a point within 120 feet of yourself.
>
> Choose one of the following damage types:
>
> - Acid
> - Cold
> - Fire
> - Lightning
> - Thunder
>
> Each creature in the Sphere must make a Dexterity saving throw.
>
> On a failed save, a creature takes damage of the chosen type equal to three rolls of your Martial Arts die.
>
> On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage.

> [!class-level|monk]
> Level 11

> [!class-feature|monk]
> #### Stride of the Elements
> ##### Level 11 Warrior of the Elements Feature
>
> While your Elemental Attunement is active, you gain:
>
> - A Fly Speed equal to your Speed.
> - A Swim Speed equal to your Speed.

> [!class-level|monk]
> Level 17

> [!class-feature|monk]
> #### Elemental Epitome
> ##### Level 17 Warrior of the Elements Feature
>
> While your Elemental Attunement is active, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Damage Resistance.** You gain Resistance to Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder damage of your choice. At the start of each of your turns, you can change the chosen damage type.
>
> - **Destructive Stride.** When you use Step of the Wind, your Speed increases by 20 feet until the end of the turn. During that movement, any creature of your choice takes damage equal to one roll of your Martial Arts die when you enter a space within 5 feet of it.
>
>   The damage type is your choice of Acid, Cold, Fire, Lightning, or Thunder.
>
>   A creature can take this damage only once per turn.
>
> - **Empowered Strikes.** Once on each of your turns, when you hit a creature with an Unarmed Strike, you can deal extra damage equal to one roll of your Martial Arts die. The extra damage is the same type dealt by the strike.
