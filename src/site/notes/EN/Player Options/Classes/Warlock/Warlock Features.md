> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Eldritch Invocations
> ##### Level 1 Warlock Feature
>
> You have unearthed Eldritch Invocations, pieces of forbidden knowledge that imbue you with magical abilities and occult secrets.
>
> You gain one invocation of your choice, such as Pact of the Tome. Invocations are described in the Eldritch Invocation Options section.
>
> **Prerequisites.** If an invocation has a prerequisite, you must meet it before selecting it.
>
> **Replacing and Gaining Invocations.** Whenever you gain a Warlock level, you can replace one invocation you know with another for which you qualify.
>
> You gain additional invocations as shown in the Eldritch Invocations column of the Warlock Features table.
>
> You can’t select the same invocation more than once unless its description says otherwise.

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Pact Magic
> ##### Level 1 Warlock Feature
>
> Through occult ceremony, you have formed a pact with a mysterious entity that grants you magical power.
>
> **Cantrips.** You know two Warlock cantrips of your choice.
>
> Whenever you gain a Warlock level, you can replace one of your cantrips from this feature with another Warlock cantrip.
>
> When you reach Warlock levels 4 and 10, you learn another Warlock cantrip.
>
> **Spell Slots.** The Warlock Features table shows how many Pact Magic spell slots you possess. All Pact Magic spell slots are the same level.
>
> You regain all expended Pact Magic spell slots when you finish a Short Rest or Long Rest.
>
> **Prepared Spells of Level 1+.** You prepare a number of Warlock spells as shown in the Prepared Spells column of the Warlock Features table.
>
> Whenever that number increases, choose additional Warlock spells until your prepared list matches the number shown in the table.
>
> If another Warlock feature grants you spells that are always prepared, those spells don't count against your prepared spells.
>
> **Changing Your Prepared Spells.** Whenever you gain a Warlock level, you can replace one spell on your list with another eligible Warlock spell.
>
> **Spellcasting Ability.** Charisma is your spellcasting ability for your Warlock spells.
>
> **Spellcasting Focus.** You can use an Arcane Focus as a Spellcasting Focus for your Warlock spells.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 2

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Magical Cunning
> ##### Level 2 Warlock Feature
>
> You can perform an esoteric rite for 1 minute.
>
> At the end of the rite, you regain expended Pact Magic spell slots equal to up to half your maximum number of Pact Magic spell slots (round up).
>
> Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Long Rest.

> Level 3

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Warlock Subclass
> ##### Level 3 Warlock Feature
>
> You gain a Warlock subclass (patron) of your choice.
>
> The following Warlock Patrons are available:

> - [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Warlock/Warlock Subclasses/Celestial Patron\|Celestial Patron]]
> - [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Warlock/Warlock Subclasses/Fiend Patron\|Fiend Patron]]
> - [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Warlock/Warlock Subclasses/Great Old One Patron\|Great Old One Patron]]
> - [[EN/Player Options/Classes/Warlock/Warlock Subclasses/Hexblade Patron\|Hexblade Patron]]

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 4

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Ability Score Improvement
> ##### Level 4 Warlock Feature
>
> You gain the Ability Score Improvement feat or another feat of your choice for which you qualify.
>
> You gain this feature again at Warlock levels 8, 12, and 16.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 9

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Contact Patron
> ##### Level 9 Warlock Feature
>
> You can now communicate directly with your patron.
>
> You always have [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Contact Other Plane\|Contact Other Plane]] prepared.
>
> You can cast it without expending a spell slot when contacting your patron, and you automatically succeed on the spell’s saving throw.
>
> Once you cast the spell this way, you can’t do so again until you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 11

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Mystic Arcanum
> ##### Level 11 Warlock Feature
>
> Your patron grants you a magical secret known as an Arcanum.
>
> Choose one level 6 Warlock spell as your Mystic Arcanum.
>
> You can cast that spell once without expending a spell slot, and you regain that use when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> You gain additional Arcanum spells at:
>
> - Level 13 (Level 7 Spell)
> - Level 15 (Level 8 Spell)
> - Level 17 (Level 9 Spell)
>
> Whenever you gain a Warlock level, you can replace one Arcanum spell with another Warlock spell of the same level.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 19

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Epic Boon
> ##### Level 19 Warlock Feature
>
> You gain an Epic Boon feat or another feat of your choice for which you qualify.
>
> Boon of Spell Recall is recommended.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 20

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Eldritch Master
> ##### Level 20 Warlock Feature
>
> When you use your Magical Cunning feature, you regain all your expended Pact Magic spell slots.
