> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Hexblade's Curse
> ##### Level 3 Hexblade Patron Feature
>
> Your patron grants you the power to curse and hinder your foes.
>
> As a Bonus Action, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself.
>
> The target is cursed for 1 minute, during which you gain the benefits below.
>
> The curse ends early if you use this feature again, dismiss it (no action required), or die.
>
> You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> When you cast a spell using a spell slot that curses a target, you can use Hexblade's Curse as part of casting that spell instead of taking a Bonus Action.
>
> When you do so, the target of the spell becomes the target of your Hexblade's Curse, and the curse's duration is either 1 minute or the spell's duration, whichever is longer.
>
> - **Hungering Hex.** When the target cursed by your Hexblade's Curse drops to 0 Hit Points, you regain Hit Points equal to 1d8 plus your Charisma modifier.
>
> - **Accursed Shield.** While you aren't wearing armor or wielding a Shield, you gain a +2 bonus to AC while you are within 10 feet of the target cursed by your Hexblade's Curse.

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Unyielding Will
> ##### Level 3 Hexblade Patron Feature
>
> When you succeed on a saving throw to maintain Concentration, each creature of your choice in a 10-foot Emanation originating from you takes 2d6 Necrotic damage.
>
> Once you use this benefit, you can't do so again until the start of your next turn.
>
> In addition, when you fail a saving throw to maintain Concentration, you can choose to succeed instead.
>
> When you do so, you gain Temporary Hit Points equal to 1d10 plus your Warlock level.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can't do so again until you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Malign Brutality
> ##### Level 6 Hexblade Patron Feature
>
> Your patron's might furthers your bloodthirst, granting the following benefits:
>
> - **Harrowing Hex.** After you cast a level 1+ spell that has a casting time of an action, you can make one attack with a weapon as a Bonus Action.
>
> - **Hindering Curse.** When you hit the target cursed by your Hexblade's Curse with an attack roll, the target has Disadvantage on the next saving throw it makes before the start of your next turn.
>
> - **Inescapable Hex.** When the target of your Hexblade's Curse ends its turn 30 feet or farther from you, you can move up to your Speed straight toward the target.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Armor of Hexes
> ##### Level 10 Hexblade Patron Feature
>
> When you take damage from the target cursed by your Hexblade's Curse, you can take a Reaction to reduce the damage taken by an amount equal to your Warlock level.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Masterful Hex
> ##### Level 14 Hexblade Patron Feature
>
> The power of your Hexblade's Curse increases, granting the following benefits:
>
> - **Accursed Critical.** Any attack roll you make against the target cursed by your Hexblade's Curse scores a Critical Hit on a roll of 19 or 20 on the d20.
>
> - **Explosive Hex.** When you deal damage to the target cursed by your Hexblade's Curse, you can cause your curse to explode with sinister energy.
>
>   The target and each creature of your choice in a 30-foot Emanation originating from the target take 3d6 Necrotic, Psychic, or Radiant damage (your choice), and their Speed is reduced by 10 feet until the start of your next turn.
>
>   Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest unless you expend a Pact Magic slot (no action required) to restore your use of it.
>
> - **Hex Restoration.** You regain one expended use of Hexblade's Curse when you finish a Short Rest.
