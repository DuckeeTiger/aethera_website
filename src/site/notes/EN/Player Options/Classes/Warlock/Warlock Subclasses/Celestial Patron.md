> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Healing Light
> ##### Level 3 Celestial Patron Feature
>
> You gain the ability to channel celestial energy to heal wounds.
>
> You have a pool of d6s used to fuel this healing. The number of dice in the pool equals 1 plus your Warlock level.
>
> As a Bonus Action, you can heal yourself or one creature you can see within 60 feet of yourself by expending dice from the pool.
>
> The maximum number of dice you can expend at one time equals your Charisma modifier (minimum of one die).
>
> Roll the expended dice and restore Hit Points equal to the total rolled.
>
> You regain all expended dice when you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Radiant Soul
> ##### Level 6 Celestial Patron Feature
>
> Your connection to your patron allows you to serve as a conduit for celestial energy.
>
> You gain Resistance to Radiant damage.
>
> In addition, once per turn when a spell you cast deals Radiant or Fire damage, you can add your Charisma modifier to one damage roll against one of the spell's targets.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Celestial Resilience
> ##### Level 10 Celestial Patron Feature
>
> You gain Temporary Hit Points whenever you use your Magical Cunning feature or finish a Short or Long Rest.
>
> These Temporary Hit Points equal your Warlock level plus your Charisma modifier.
>
> In addition, choose up to five creatures you can see when you gain these Temporary Hit Points.
>
> Each chosen creature gains Temporary Hit Points equal to half your Warlock level plus your Charisma modifier.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Searing Vengeance
> ##### Level 14 Celestial Patron Feature
>
> When you or an ally within 60 feet of you is about to make a Death Saving Throw, you can unleash radiant energy to preserve that creature's life.
>
> The creature regains Hit Points equal to half its Hit Point maximum and can immediately end the [[EN/Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Prone\|Prone]] condition on itself.
>
> Each creature of your choice within 30 feet of that creature takes Radiant damage equal to 2d8 plus your Charisma modifier and has the [[EN/Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Blinded\|Blinded]] condition until the end of the current turn.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
