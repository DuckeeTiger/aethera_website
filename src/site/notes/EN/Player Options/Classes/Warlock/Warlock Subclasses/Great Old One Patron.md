> [!class-recite|warlock]
> *Unearth Forbidden Lore of Ineffable Beings*
>
> Your pact reaches beyond mortal understanding, drawing power from entities whose motives are unknowable.
>
> You may have bound yourself to a being of the Far Realm, an elder god such as Hadar or Tharizdun, or countless alien intelligences whose whispers seep through reality.
>
> These entities may scarcely notice your existence.
>
> Yet the fragments of their forbidden knowledge grant you strange and unsettling powers.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Awakened Mind
> ##### Level 3 Great Old One Patron Feature
>
> You can form a telepathic connection between your mind and another creature's.
>
> As a Bonus Action, choose one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself.
>
> You and the chosen creature can communicate telepathically while you remain within a number of miles of one another equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of 1 mile).
>
> To understand each other, both participants must mentally use a language the other knows.
>
> The connection lasts for a number of minutes equal to your Warlock level.
>
> It ends early if you use this feature to connect with a different creature.

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Psychic Spells
> ##### Level 3 Great Old One Patron Feature
>
> When you cast a Warlock spell that deals damage, you can change its damage type to Psychic.
>
> In addition, when you cast a Warlock spell from the Enchantment or Illusion school, you can cast it without Verbal or Somatic components.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Clairvoyant Combatant
> ##### Level 6 Great Old One Patron Feature
>
> When you form a telepathic bond with a creature using your Awakened Mind feature, you can force that creature to make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC.
>
> On a failed save:
>
> - The creature has Disadvantage on attack rolls against you.
> - You have Advantage on attack rolls against that creature.
>
> These effects last for the duration of the telepathic bond.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.
>
> You can also restore a use by expending a Pact Magic spell slot (no action required).

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Eldritch Hex
> ##### Level 10 Great Old One Patron Feature
>
> Your alien patron grants you a particularly potent curse.
>
> You always have [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Hex\|Hex]] prepared.
>
> Whenever you cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Hex\|Hex]] and choose an ability score, the target also has Disadvantage on saving throws made with that chosen ability for the duration of the spell.

> [!class-divider|warlock small]
> ---

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Thought Shield
> ##### Level 10 Great Old One Patron Feature
>
> Your thoughts cannot be read by telepathy or other magical means unless you willingly allow it.
>
> You also gain Resistance to Psychic damage.
>
> Whenever a creature deals Psychic damage to you, that creature takes Psychic damage equal to the amount you take.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Create Thrall
> ##### Level 14 Great Old One Patron Feature
>
> When you cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Summon Aberration\|Summon Aberration]], you can modify the spell so that it doesn't require Concentration.
>
> If you do so, the spell's duration becomes 1 minute for that casting.
>
> In addition, the summoned Aberration gains Temporary Hit Points equal to your Warlock level plus your Charisma modifier.
>
> Furthermore, the first time each turn the Aberration hits a creature affected by your [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Hex\|Hex]], it deals extra Psychic damage equal to the bonus damage granted by that spell.
