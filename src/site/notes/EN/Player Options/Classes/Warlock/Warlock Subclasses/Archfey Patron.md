> [!class-recite|warlock]
> *Bargain with Whimsical Fey*
>
> Your pact draws on the power of the Feywild.
>
> You may have forged a bargain with an Archfey such as the Prince of Frost, Titania of the Summer Court, the Queen of Air and Darkness, or an ancient hag.
>
> Others weave a web of debts, favors, and obligations among countless Fey spirits.
>
> Whatever the source of your pact, your patron is often inscrutable, capricious, and delightfully unpredictable.

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Steps of the Fey
> ##### Level 3 Archfey Patron Feature
>
> Your patron grants you the ability to move between the boundaries of the planes.
>
> You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Misty Step\|Misty Step]] without expending a spell slot a number of times equal to your Charisma modifier (minimum of once).
>
> You regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> Whenever you cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Misty Step\|Misty Step]], you can choose one of the following additional effects:
>
> - **Refreshing Step.** Immediately after you teleport, you or one creature you can see within 10 feet of yourself gains 1d10 Temporary Hit Points.
>
> - **Taunting Step.** Creatures within 5 feet of the space you left must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or have Disadvantage on attack rolls against creatures other than you until the start of your next turn.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Misty Escape
> ##### Level 6 Archfey Patron Feature
>
> You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Misty Step\|Misty Step]] as a Reaction when you take damage.
>
> In addition, the following options become available for your Steps of the Fey feature:
>
> - **Disappearing Step.** You gain the Invisible condition until the start of your next turn or until immediately after you make an attack roll, deal damage, or cast a spell.
>
> - **Dreadful Step.** Creatures within 5 feet of either the space you left or the space you appear in (your choice) must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC or take 2d10 Psychic damage.

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Beguiling Defenses
> ##### Level 10 Archfey Patron Feature
>
> Your patron teaches you how to shield both mind and body.
>
> You are immune to the [[EN/Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Charmed\|Charmed]] condition.
>
> In addition, immediately after a creature you can see hits you with an attack roll, you can take a Reaction to reduce the damage you take by half (round down).
>
> You can then force the attacker to make a Wisdom saving throw against your spell save DC.
>
> On a failed save, the attacker takes Psychic damage equal to the damage you take.
>
> Once you use this Reaction, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
>
> You can also restore a use by expending a Pact Magic spell slot (no action required).

> [!class-level|warlock]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|warlock]
> #### Bewitching Magic
> ##### Level 14 Archfey Patron Feature
>
> Your patron grants you the ability to weave teleportation into your spellcasting.
>
> Immediately after you cast an Enchantment or Illusion spell using both an action and a spell slot, you can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Misty Step\|Misty Step]] as part of the same action without expending a spell slot.
