> [!class-recite|rogue]
> *Embrace Death and Wield Ghostly Power*
>
> Some Rogues traverse the veil between life and death, shepherding opponents to the grave and slipping through the world as silently as a spirit.
>
> Their connection to death infuses their strikes with necrotic power and allows them to steal knowledge from departed souls.
>
> Thieves’ guilds and spies prize Phantoms for their uncanny ability to gather secrets from both the living and the dead.

> [!class-level|rogue]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Wails from the Grave
> ##### Level 3 Phantom Feature
>
> Immediately after you deal Sneak Attack damage to a creature on your turn, you can target a second creature you can see within 30 feet of the first creature.
>
> Roll a number of Sneak Attack dice equal to half your Sneak Attack dice for your level (round up).
>
> The second creature takes Necrotic damage equal to the total rolled as spectral wails echo around it.
>
> You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Dexterity modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Whispers of the Dead
> ##### Level 3 Phantom Feature
>
> Whenever you finish a Short or Long Rest, you can choose one skill or tool proficiency that you lack and gain proficiency with it.
>
> A ghostly presence shares its knowledge with you.
>
> You lose this proficiency when you use this feature again to choose a different one.

> [!class-level|rogue]
> Level 9

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Tokens of the Departed
> ##### Level 9 Phantom Feature
>
> The spirits of the dead are drawn to you, manifesting as strange soul trinkets infused with lingering echoes of life.
>
> You gain two soul trinkets.
>
> A soul trinket is a Tiny object whose appearance is determined by you or the DM.
>
> If you move more than 30 feet from a trinket, it immediately teleports back to your person.
>
> You can use soul trinkets in the following ways:
>
> - **Death’s Knell.** When you deal Sneak Attack damage, you can destroy one soul trinket and immediately use Wails from the Grave without expending a use of that feature.
>
> - **Life Essence.** While you possess at least one soul trinket, you have Advantage on Death Saving Throws and Constitution saving throws.
>
> - **Spirit Query.** As a Magic action, you can destroy one soul trinket to cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Augury\|Augury]] without spell components, using Constitution as your spellcasting ability.
>
>   If you know the creature associated with the trinket, you may instead ask its spirit one question. The spirit appears and answers as concisely as possible in a language it knew in life.
>
> **Gaining Additional Soul Trinkets.**
>
> When a creature you can see within 30 feet of you dies, you can take a Reaction to gain a new soul trinket.
>
> You can have a maximum of two soul trinkets at a time.
>
> If you gain another while at maximum capacity, one existing trinket is destroyed and replaced.
>
> The maximum number of trinkets increases to three at Rogue level 13 and four at Rogue level 17.
>
> Whenever you finish a Long Rest with fewer than two soul trinkets, you gain enough to return to two.

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Voice of Death
> ##### Level 9 Phantom Feature
>
> You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Speak with Dead\|Speak with Dead]] once without expending a spell slot.
>
> The spell requires no material components and uses Dexterity as its spellcasting ability.
>
> You regain the ability to cast it in this way when you finish a Short or Long Rest.
>
> When you cast the spell, you can target one of your soul trinkets instead of a corpse, allowing the associated spirit to answer your questions.

> [!class-level|rogue]
> Level 13

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Ghost Walk
> ##### Level 13 Phantom Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you assume a spectral form for 10 minutes.
>
> The effect ends early if you dismiss it (no action required).
>
> Once you use this feature, you can't do so again until you finish a Long Rest unless you destroy one soul trinket from Tokens of the Departed to restore its use.
>
> While in this form, you gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Flight.** You gain a Fly Speed of 10 feet and can hover.
>
> - **Hazy Form.** Attack rolls against you have Disadvantage.
>
> - **Incorporeal Movement.** You can move through creatures and objects as though they were Difficult Terrain.
>
>   If you end your turn inside a creature or object, you take 1d10 Force damage.

> [!class-level|rogue]
> Level 17

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Death’s Friend
> ##### Level 17 Phantom Feature
>
> Your bond with death grows stronger, granting the following benefits:
>
> - **Death’s Lament.** When you use Wails from the Grave, you can deal the feature's Necrotic damage to both the original target and the secondary target.
>
> - **Draw of Death.** When you roll Initiative, if you have no remaining soul trinkets, you immediately gain one soul trinket for your Tokens of the Departed feature.
