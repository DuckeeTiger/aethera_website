> [!class-recite|rogue]
> *Become a Gruesome Agent of Malice*
>
> A Scion of the Three draws power from the Dead Three:
>
> - **Bane**, god of tyranny
> - **Bhaal**, god of murder
> - **Myrkul**, god of death
>
> Whether through devotion, curse, or dark fate, these Rogues wield sinister gifts and strike fear into their enemies with supernatural brutality.

> [!class-level|rogue]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Bloodthirst
> ##### Level 3 Scion of the Three Feature
>
> When an enemy you can see within 30 feet of yourself takes damage and becomes Bloodied from that damage without being reduced to 0 Hit Points, you can take a Reaction to teleport to an unoccupied space you can see within 5 feet of that creature.
>
> You can then make one melee attack against it.
>
> You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Intelligence modifier (minimum of once), and regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-divider|rogue small]
> ---

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Dread Allegiance
> ##### Level 3 Scion of the Three Feature
>
> Choose one of the Dead Three: **Bane**, **Bhaal**, or **Myrkul**.
>
> You gain the associated damage Resistance and cantrip listed below.
>
> Intelligence is your spellcasting ability for this cantrip.
>
> Whenever you finish a Long Rest, you can change your choice.
>
> | God | Damage Resistance | Cantrip |
> |---|---|---|
> | **Bane** | Psychic | [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Minor Illusion\|Minor Illusion]] |
> | **Bhaal** | Poison | [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Blade Ward\|Blade Ward]] |
> | **Myrkul** | Necrotic | [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Chill Touch\|Chill Touch]] |

> [!class-level|rogue]
> Level 9

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Strike Fear
> ##### Level 9 Scion of the Three Feature
>
> You gain the following Cunning Strike option:
>
> **Terrify (Cost: 1d6).**
>
> The target must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw or gain the Frightened condition for 1 minute.
>
> While the target is Frightened in this way, you have Advantage on attack rolls against it.
>
> The target repeats the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on a success.

> [!class-level|rogue]
> Level 13

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Aura of Malevolence
> ##### Level 13 Scion of the Three Feature
>
> You radiate malignant power associated with your chosen allegiance.
>
> Whenever you use Bloodthirst and teleport, each creature of your choice within 10 feet of either:
>
> - the space you left, or
> - the space you teleport to
>
> (your choice) takes damage equal to your Intelligence modifier.
>
> The damage type matches the Resistance granted by your Dread Allegiance feature.
>
> Damage dealt by this feature ignores Resistance.

> [!class-level|rogue]
> Level 17

> [!class-feature|rogue]
> #### Dread Incarnate
> ##### Level 17 Scion of the Three Feature
>
> You gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Cutthroat.** When you finish a Short Rest, you regain one expended use of Bloodthirst.
>
> - **Murderous Intent.** When you roll Sneak Attack damage, any Sneak Attack die that rolls a 1 or 2 is treated as a 3.
