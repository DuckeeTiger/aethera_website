> [!class-recite|bard]
> *Call Forth Spirits from Beyond the Grave*
>
> Bards of the College of Spirits conjure legendary spirits to influence the world around them.
>
> The entities they channel are powerful but capricious, and what a Bard summons is not always entirely under their control.
>
> Through stories, omens, and whispered voices from beyond the veil, these Bards serve as intermediaries between the living and the dead.

> [!class-level|bard]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|bard]
> #### Channeler
> ##### Level 3 College of Spirits Feature
>
> You learn how to contact spirits beyond the grave, allowing their power and knowledge to flow through you.
>
> You gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Guiding Whispers.** You know the [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Guidance\|Guidance]] cantrip. Its range becomes 60 feet when you cast it.
>
> - **Spiritual Focus.** You gain a Gaming Set (playing cards) and proficiency with it.
>
>   You can use the cards, an Arcane Focus (crystal or orb), a candle, or an ink pen as a Spellcasting Focus for your Bard spells.

> [!class-feature|bard]
> #### Spirits from Beyond
> ##### Level 3 College of Spirits Feature
>
> You can call forth spirits of the dead to empower allies or hinder enemies.
>
> Whenever you use a Bonus Action to grant a creature a Bardic Inspiration die, roll that die and consult the table below. The resulting spirit becomes channeled until you unleash it or finish a Short or Long Rest.
>
> **Controlled Channeling.** As a Bonus Action, you can expend a use of Bardic Inspiration to choose a spirit instead of rolling.
>
> The chosen spirit's number must be equal to or lower than the highest value on your Bardic Inspiration die.
>
> **Unleashing a Spirit.** As a Magic action, choose a creature you can see within 30 feet. The spirit takes effect on that target.
>
> If a spirit requires a saving throw, the DC equals your Bard spell save DC.
>

>> | 3 | Avenger | Until the end of your next turn, creatures that hit the target with melee attacks take Force damage equal to one Bardic Inspiration die. |
>> | 4 | Renegade | Target can immediately use its Reaction to teleport up to 30 feet. |
>> | 5 | Fortune Teller | Target has Advantage on D20 Tests until the start of your next turn. |
>> | 6 | Wayfarer | Target gains Temporary HP equal to one Bardic Inspiration die + your Bard level and gains +10 Speed while those HP remain. |
>> | 7 | Trickster | Wisdom save or take Psychic damage equal to two Bardic Inspiration dice and become [[EN/Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Charmed\|Charmed]] until the start of your next turn. Half damage on a success. |
>> | 8 | Shade | Target becomes Invisible until the end of its next turn or until it attacks, deals damage, or casts a spell. When invisibility ends, nearby creatures take Necrotic damage on a failed Constitution save. |
>> | 9 | Arsonist | Dexterity save or take Fire damage equal to four Bardic Inspiration dice. Half damage on a success. |
>> | 10 | Coward | Wisdom save or become [[EN/Player Options/Rules/Conditions/Frightened\|Frightened]] until the start of your next turn. While frightened in this way, Speed is halved and the creature can take either an Action or Bonus Action, not both. |

> Level 6

> [!class-feature|bard]
> #### Empowered Channeling
> ##### Level 6 College of Spirits Feature
>
> Your mastery over spiritual forces grows stronger.
>
> You gain the following benefits:
>
> - **Power from Beyond.** Once per turn when you cast a Bard spell with a spell slot that deals damage or restores Hit Points, roll 1d6.
>
>   Add the result to one damage roll of the spell or to the total Hit Points restored by the spell.
>
> - **Spiritual Manifestation.** You always have [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Spirit Guardians\|Spirit Guardians]] prepared.
>
>   You can cast it once without expending a spell slot, regaining that use when you finish a Long Rest.
>
>   Whenever you cast the spell, you can empower it so that you and your allies within its Emanation gain Half Cover.
>
>   Once you use this modification, you can't do so again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.

> [!class-level|bard]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|bard]
> #### Mystical Connection
> ##### Level 14 College of Spirits Feature
>
> You gain greater mastery over the spirits you channel.
>
> Whenever you roll on the Spirits from Beyond table, roll twice and choose which result to use.
>
> If both rolls are the same number, you can instead choose any spirit from the table.
