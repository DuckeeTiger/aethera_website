This section presents the Bard spell list.
The spells are organized by spell level and then alphabetized, and each spell’s school of magic is listed.

In the Special column:

- **C** means the spell requires Concentration.
- **R** means the spell is a Ritual.
- **M** means the spell requires a specific Material component.
