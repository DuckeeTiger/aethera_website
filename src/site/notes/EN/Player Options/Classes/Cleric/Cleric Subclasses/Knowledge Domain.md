> [!class-recite|cleric]
> *Unearth Secrets and Master the Mind*
>
> The Knowledge Domain values learning and understanding above all.
>
> Clerics who tap into this domain study esoteric lore, collect ancient tomes, delve into secret places, and examine the workings of the mind.
>
> To them, knowledge is more valuable than material wealth, and learning itself is an act of worship.
>
> Libraries, universities, and institutions of learning often draw upon the power of this domain.

> [!class-level|cleric]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|cleric]
> #### Blessings of Knowledge
> ##### Level 3 Knowledge Domain Feature
>
> You gain proficiency with one type of Artisan's Tools of your choice.
>
> In addition, choose two of the following skills:
>
> - Arcana
> - History
> - Nature
> - Religion
>
> You gain proficiency in the chosen skills and Expertise with both of them.

> [!class-feature|cleric]
> #### Mind Magic
> ##### Level 3 Knowledge Domain Feature
>
> As a Magic action, you can expend one use of your Channel Divinity to manifest your magical knowledge.
>
> Choose one Divination spell from your Knowledge Domain Spells feature that you have prepared.
>
> As part of the same action, you cast that spell without expending a spell slot and without requiring Material components.

> [!class-level|cleric]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|cleric]
> #### Unfettered Mind
> ##### Level 6 Knowledge Domain Feature
>
> You gain telepathy with a range of 60 feet.
>
> When using this telepathy, you can simultaneously communicate with a number of creatures equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of one creature).
>
> In addition, you gain proficiency in Intelligence saving throws.
>
> If you already have proficiency in Intelligence saving throws, you instead gain proficiency in one saving throw of your choice that you currently lack.

> [!class-level|cleric]
> Level 17

> [!class-feature|cleric]
> #### Divine Foreknowledge
> ##### Level 17 Knowledge Domain Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you magically expand your mind into the future.
>
> For 1 hour, you have Advantage on all D20 Tests.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
>
> You can also restore its use by expending a level 6+ spell slot (no action required).
