> [!class-recite|cleric]
> *Embody Deific Forces of Death*
>
> The Grave Domain concerns itself with the boundary between life and death.
>
> To those who tap into this domain’s power, death is a natural and inevitable part of the multiverse. Such Clerics seek to destroy undead and shepherd wandering spirits.
>
> Though they can stave off death for a time, they understand that the grave will always claim its due.

> [!class-feature|cleric]
> #### Circle of Mortality
> ##### Level 3 Grave Domain Feature
>
> You can manipulate the balance between life and death, granting the following benefits:
>
> - **Pull of Death.** Once per turn, when you deal damage to a creature that is missing any Hit Points by casting a spell or hitting with an attack roll, the creature takes an extra 1d4 Necrotic damage.
>
>   This extra damage increases to 1d6 when you reach Cleric level 11.
>
> - **Return to Life.** You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Spare the Dying\|Spare the Dying]] as a Bonus Action.
>
>   Additionally, when you would normally roll one or more dice to restore Hit Points to a creature at 0 Hit Points with a spell or Channel Divinity, you instead use the maximum value for each die.
>
>   For example, instead of restoring 2d4 Hit Points, you restore 8.

> [!class-feature|cleric]
> #### Path to the Grave
> ##### Level 3 Grave Domain Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you present your Holy Symbol and expend a use of Channel Divinity to curse one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself until the start of your next turn.
>
> While cursed, the creature has Disadvantage on attack rolls and saving throws.
>
> When you or an ally you can see hits the cursed creature with an attack roll, you can end the curse early (no action required) to cause the attack to deal extra Necrotic or Radiant damage (your choice) equal to your Cleric level.

> [!class-level|cleric]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|cleric]
> #### Sentinel at Death’s Door
> ##### Level 6 Grave Domain Feature
>
> When you or a Bloodied creature you can see within 60 feet of yourself is hit by an attack roll, you can take a Reaction to halve the attack’s damage (round down).
>
> If the triggering attack was a Critical Hit, any effects triggered by that Critical Hit are canceled.
>
> You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Wisdom modifier (minimum of once), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

> [!class-level|cleric]
> Level 17

> [!class-feature|cleric]
> #### Divine Reaper
> ##### Level 17 Grave Domain Feature
>
> Your deep connection to death grants you the following benefits:
>
> - **Enhanced Necromancy.** When you cast a level 5 or lower spell from the Necromancy school that targets one creature, or when you cast a spell from your Grave Domain Spells feature, you can expend a use of Channel Divinity to target a second creature within the spell’s range.
>
>   If the spell requires costly or consumed Material components, you must provide them separately for each target.
>
> - **Keeper of Souls.** When an enemy dies within 60 feet of you, you or one creature you can see within 60 feet regains Hit Points equal to twice your Cleric level.
>
>   You can't use this benefit while Incapacitated.
>
>   Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Short or Long Rest unless you expend a level 6+ spell slot (no action required) to restore its use.
