> [!class-recite|sorcerer]
> *A Dazzling Mage Filled with Innate Magic*
>
> Sorcerers wield innate magic that is stamped into their being. Some Sorcerers can’t name the origin of their power, while others trace it to strange events in their personal or family history.
>
> The blessing of a dragon or a dryad at a baby’s birth, a brush with extraplanar forces, or even a glimpse into the hidden workings of reality might awaken a Sorcerer’s gift.
>
> Sorcerers don’t learn magic in the same way Wizards do. The raw power of magic is already part of them, and mastering that power means learning to shape, direct, and unleash it.
>
> As Sorcerers grow stronger, they become increasingly attuned to the source of their power, developing abilities that reflect the magical force flowing through their blood and soul.

> ## Becoming a Sorcerer

> [!class-header|sorcerer]
> #### As a Level 1 Character
- Gain all the traits in the Core Sorcerer Traits table.
- Gain the Sorcerer's level 1 features, which are listed in the Sorcerer Features table.

> [!class-header|sorcerer]
> #### As a Multiclass Character
- Gain the Hit Point Die trait from the Core Sorcerer Traits table.
- Gain the Sorcerer's level 1 features, which are listed in the Sorcerer Features table.
- See the multiclassing rules to determine your available spell slots.
