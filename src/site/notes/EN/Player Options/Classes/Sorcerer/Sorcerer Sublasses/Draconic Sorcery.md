> [!class-recite|sorcerer]
> *Breathe the Magic of Dragons*
>
> Your innate magic comes from the gift of a dragon.
>
> Perhaps an ancient dragon facing death bequeathed some of its magical power to you or your ancestor.
>
> You might have absorbed magic from a site infused with draconic power, handled a relic from a dragon's hoard, or even carry draconic blood within your lineage.
>
> Whatever its source, draconic magic courses through your body and soul.

> [!class-level|sorcerer]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|sorcerer]
> #### Draconic Resilience
> ##### Level 3 Draconic Sorcery Feature
>
> The magic in your body manifests physical traits of your draconic gift.
>
> Your Hit Point maximum increases by 3, and it increases by 1 whenever you gain another Sorcerer level.
>
> Parts of your body are also covered by dragon-like scales.
>
> While you aren't wearing armor, your base Armor Class equals 10 plus your Dexterity modifier and your Charisma modifier.

> [!class-feature|sorcerer]
> #### Elemental Affinity
> ##### Level 6 Draconic Sorcery Feature
>
> Your draconic magic has an affinity with a damage type associated with dragons.
>
> Choose one of the following damage types:
>
> - Acid
> - Cold
> - Fire
> - Lightning
> - Poison
>
> You gain Resistance to the chosen damage type.
>
> In addition, whenever you cast a spell that deals damage of that type, you can add your Charisma modifier to one damage roll of that spell.

> [!class-level|sorcerer]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|sorcerer]
> #### Dragon Wings
> ##### Level 14 Draconic Sorcery Feature
>
> As a Bonus Action, you can cause draconic wings to appear on your back.
>
> The wings last for 1 hour or until you dismiss them (no action required).
>
> While they are present, you have a Fly Speed of 60 feet.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Long Rest unless you spend 3 Sorcery Points (no action required) to restore your use of it.

> [!class-level|sorcerer]
> Level 18

> [!class-feature|sorcerer]
> #### Dragon Companion
> ##### Level 18 Draconic Sorcery Feature
>
> You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Summon Dragon\|Summon Dragon]] without a Material component.
>
> You can also cast the spell once without expending a spell slot, and you regain the ability to do so when you finish a Long Rest.
>
> Whenever you begin casting the spell, you can modify it so that it doesn't require Concentration.
>
> If you do so, the spell's duration becomes 1 minute for that casting.
