> [!class-recite|sorcerer]
> *Wield Raw Magic*
>
> Your innate power stems from the source of magic itself: the Weave.
>
> This connection manifests as a rare ability known as spellfire, and you surge with radiant bursts of raw magical energy.
>
> Your talent with spellfire allows you to heal allies, sear enemies, and absorb powerful spells.
>
> Wielders of spellfire often wander the world in pursuit of knowledge, adventure, and mastery over their remarkable gift.

> [!class-level|sorcerer]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|sorcerer]
> #### Spellfire Burst
> ##### Level 3 Spellfire Sorcery Feature
>
> When you spend at least 1 Sorcery Point as part of a Magic action or a Bonus Action on your turn, you can unleash one of the following magical effects of your choice.
>
> You can do so only once per turn.
>
> - **Bolstering Flames.** You or one creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself gains Temporary Hit Points equal to 1d4 plus your Charisma modifier.
>
> - **Radiant Fire.** One creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself takes 1d4 Fire or Radiant damage (your choice).

> [!class-feature|sorcerer]
> #### Absorb Spells
> ##### Level 6 Spellfire Sorcery Feature
>
> You always have [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Counterspell\|Counterspell]] prepared.
>
> In addition, whenever a target fails the saving throw against a [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/Counterspell\|Counterspell]] you cast, you regain 1d4 Sorcery Points.

> [!class-level|sorcerer]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|sorcerer]
> #### Honed Spellfire
> ##### Level 14 Spellfire Sorcery Feature
>
> Your Spellfire Burst improves.
>
> - **Bolstering Flames.** Add your Sorcerer level to the Temporary Hit Points granted.
> - **Radiant Fire.** The damage increases from 1d4 to 1d8.

> [!class-level|sorcerer]
> Level 18

> [!class-feature|sorcerer]
> #### Crown of Spellfire
> ##### Level 18 Spellfire Sorcery Feature
>
> When you use Innate Sorcery, you can alter it and infuse yourself with the essence of spellfire, gaining the following benefits while that use of Innate Sorcery remains active.
>
> Once you use this feature to alter Innate Sorcery, you can't do so again until you finish a Long Rest unless you spend 5 Sorcery Points (no action required) to restore your use of it.
>
> - **Burning Life Force.** Once per turn when you are hit by an attack roll, you can expend a number of Hit Point Dice up to your Charisma modifier (minimum of one die). Roll the expended dice and reduce the damage taken by the total rolled.
>
> - **Flight.** You gain a Fly Speed of 60 feet and can hover.
>
> - **Spell Avoidance.** When you're subjected to a spell or magical effect that allows you to make a saving throw to take only half damage, you instead take no damage on a successful save and only half damage on a failed save.
>
>   You can't benefit from this feature while you have the Incapacitated condition.
