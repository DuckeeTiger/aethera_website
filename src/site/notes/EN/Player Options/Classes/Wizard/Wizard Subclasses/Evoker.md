> [!class-recite|wizard]
> *Create Explosive Elemental Effects*
>
> Your studies focus on magic that unleashes devastating elemental power.
>
> Evokers specialize in spells that conjure searing flames, crackling lightning, rolling thunder, bitter cold, and burning acid.
>
> Some serve in military forces as living artillery, while others use their mastery to defend the innocent.
>
> Whatever their calling, Evokers wield some of the most destructive magic in existence.

> [!class-level|wizard]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Evocation Savant
> ##### Level 3 Evoker Feature
>
> Choose two Wizard spells from the Evocation school, each of which must be level 2 or lower.
>
> Add those spells to your spellbook for free.
>
> In addition, whenever you gain access to a new level of spell slots in this class, you can add one Evocation spell from the Wizard spell list to your spellbook for free.
>
> The chosen spell must be of a level for which you have spell slots.

> [!class-divider|wizard small]
> ---

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Potent Cantrip
> ##### Level 3 Evoker Feature
>
> Your damaging cantrips remain dangerous even when foes evade their full effects.
>
> When you cast a cantrip that targets a creature and:
>
> - You miss with the attack roll, or
> - The target succeeds on its saving throw,
>
> the target still takes half the cantrip's damage, if any.
>
> The target suffers none of the cantrip's additional effects.

> [!class-level|wizard]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Sculpt Spells
> ##### Level 6 Evoker Feature
>
> You can shape destructive magic around your allies.
>
> Whenever you cast an Evocation spell that affects other creatures you can see, you can choose a number of those creatures equal to:
>
> **1 + the spell's level**
>
> The chosen creatures automatically succeed on their saving throws against the spell.
>
> In addition, if a successful saving throw would normally cause a creature to take half damage, those creatures instead take no damage.

> [!class-level|wizard]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Empowered Evocation
> ##### Level 10 Evoker Feature
>
> Whenever you cast a Wizard spell from the Evocation school, you can add your Intelligence modifier to one damage roll of that spell.

> [!class-level|wizard]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Overchannel
> ##### Level 14 Evoker Feature
>
> You can push your spells beyond their normal limits.
>
> When you cast a Wizard spell that:
>
> - Deals damage, and
> - Uses a spell slot of level 1 to 5,
>
> you can cause the spell to deal maximum damage instead of rolling.
>
> The first time you use this feature between Long Rests, you suffer no adverse effect.
>
> If you use this feature again before finishing a Long Rest, you immediately take **2d12 Necrotic damage per spell level** after casting the spell.
>
> This damage ignores Resistance and Immunity.
>
> Each additional use before finishing a Long Rest increases the damage by an extra **1d12 per spell level**.
>
> For example:
>
> - Second use: **2d12 per spell level**
> - Third use: **3d12 per spell level**
> - Fourth use: **4d12 per spell level**
>
> and so on.
