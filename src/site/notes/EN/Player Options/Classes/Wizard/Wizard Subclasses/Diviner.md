> [!class-recite|wizard]
> *Learn the Secrets of the Multiverse*
>
> The counsel of a Diviner is sought by those who seek a clearer understanding of the past, present, and future.
>
> As a Diviner, you strive to part the veils of space, time, and consciousness.
>
> You dedicate yourself to mastering magic that reveals hidden truths, grants supernatural knowledge, pierces distance, and offers glimpses of what has yet to come.
>
> Through study and foresight, you become one of the greatest seekers of knowledge in the multiverse.

> [!class-level|wizard]
> Level 3

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Divination Savant
> ##### Level 3 Diviner Feature
>
> Choose two Wizard spells from the Divination school, each of which must be level 2 or lower.
>
> Add those spells to your spellbook for free.
>
> In addition, whenever you gain access to a new level of spell slots in this class, you can add one Divination spell from the Wizard spell list to your spellbook for free.
>
> The chosen spell must be of a level for which you have spell slots.

> [!class-divider|wizard small]
> ---

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Portent
> ##### Level 3 Diviner Feature
>
> Glimpses of possible futures begin to intrude upon your awareness.
>
> Whenever you finish a Long Rest, roll two d20s and record the numbers rolled.
>
> You can replace any D20 Test made by you or a creature you can see with one of these foretelling rolls.
>
> You must choose to do so before the roll is made.
>
> You can replace a roll in this way only once per turn.
>
> Each foretelling roll can be used only once.
>
> When you finish a Long Rest, any unused foretelling rolls are lost.

> [!class-level|wizard]
> Level 6

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Expert Divination
> ##### Level 6 Diviner Feature
>
> Casting Divination magic requires only a fraction of your effort.
>
> Whenever you cast a Divination spell using a level 2+ spell slot, you regain one expended spell slot.
>
> The regained slot must:
>
> - Be lower in level than the slot expended.
> - Be no higher than level 5.

> [!class-level|wizard]
> Level 10

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### The Third Eye
> ##### Level 10 Diviner Feature
>
> You can enhance your perception beyond mortal limits.
>
> As a Bonus Action, choose one of the following benefits.
>
> The chosen benefit lasts until you start a Short or Long Rest.
>
> Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a Short or Long Rest.
>
> - **Darkvision.** You gain Darkvision with a range of 120 feet.
>
> - **Greater Comprehension.** You can read any language.
>
> - **See Invisibility.** You can cast [[EN/Player Options/Spells/Detailed Spells/See Invisibility\|See Invisibility]] without expending a spell slot.

> [!class-level|wizard]
> Level 14

> [!class-feature|wizard]
> #### Greater Portent
> ##### Level 14 Diviner Feature
>
> Your visions become clearer and more accurate.
>
> Whenever you roll for your Portent feature, you roll three d20s instead of two.
