> [!class-recite|wizard]
> *A Scholarly Magic-User of Arcane Power*
>
> Wizards are defined by their exhaustive study of magic’s inner workings. They cast spells of explosive fire, arcing lightning, subtle deception, and spectacular transformations.
>
> Their magic conjures monsters from other planes of existence, glimpses the future, and forms protective barriers. Their mightiest spells change one substance into another, call meteors from the sky, or open portals to other worlds.
>
> Most Wizards share a scholarly approach to magic. They examine the theoretical underpinnings of magic, particularly the categorization of spells into schools of magic.
>
> The lure of knowledge calls even the most unadventurous Wizards from the safety of their libraries and laboratories into crumbling ruins and lost cities.

> ## Becoming a Wizard

> [!class-header|wizard]
> #### As a Level 1 Character
- Gain all the traits in the Core Wizard Traits table.
- Gain the Wizard’s level 1 features, which are listed in the Wizard Features table.

> [!class-header|wizard]
> #### As a Multiclass Character
- Gain the Hit Point Die from the Core Wizard Traits table.
- Gain the Wizard’s level 1 features, which are listed in the Wizard Features table.
- See the multiclassing rules to determine your available spell slots.
