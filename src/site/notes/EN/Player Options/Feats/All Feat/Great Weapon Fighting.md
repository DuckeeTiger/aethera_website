> [!feat|no-t]
> # Great Weapon Fighting
> *Fighting Style Feat*
>
> | Prerequisite | Requirement | Source |
> |---|---|---|
> | Fighting Style Feature | No | PHB 2024 |
>
> ---
>
> You gain the following benefits: 
> - ***Great Weapon Damage.*** When you roll damage for an attack you make with a Melee weapon that you are holding with two hands, you can treat any 1 or 2 on a damage die as a 3. The weapon must have the Two-Handed or Versatile property to gain this benefit.
